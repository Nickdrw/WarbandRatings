# Warband Ratings

Inspired by a WeakAura I used back in the WeakAura era, Warband Ratings is a lightweight addon that lets you check your ratings and seasonal progression no matter which character you are currently logged into.

It provides an instant overview of your PvP and PvE progress, organized by class and character name, so you can quickly see where each of your characters stands across your warband.

## Supported data

Solo Shuffle  
Solo Battlegrounds  
2v2  
3v3  
10v10  
Honor earned  
Conquest earned and season cap  
Honorable Kills  
Mythic+  
Crests

## New features

Rating history graphs for PvP brackets  
MMR and rating curves with toggleable display  
Detached graph window for a larger view  
Column filters and display options

## How it works

When a character is logged in, the addon records and updates that character’s rating data automatically. PvP match history is also tracked over time, allowing the addon to display rating and MMR progression graphs from any character.

## Goal

The purpose of Warband Ratings is simple: make it easy to track all your characters’ ratings and PvP progress without having to log in and out repeatedly or rely on external tools.
